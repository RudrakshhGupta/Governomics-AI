ABOUT GOVERNOMICS

Building Better Public Policy with AI

Governomics combines machine learning and macroeconomic indicators to simulate the possible impact of public policies before implementation.

Our Mission

Governomics aims to assist policymakers, researchers and students by providing an AI-powered platform that estimates the potential outcomes of public policies using economic indicators and machine learning. Instead of relying only on intuition, users can compare multiple policies and make more informed decisions.
